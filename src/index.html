About
Michicant is a collaborative project by composers Kalupto and Agathe Lavarel and visual artist Anna Baumann.
The work is a symbiosis of music and partially generative visuals, which evolve in relation to the sound.
In its ecstatic wanderings, the work carries us away between frozen lake and reminiscence. From a deep-rooted, mysterious universe, we rise to the radiant sublime. Is it an island we see in the distance? An island of fading memories and lost loves.
